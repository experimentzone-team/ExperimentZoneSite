The Experimentation Methodology for Ecommerce Businesses

Before starting an experiment, make sure you understand why you're doing it. You can use the GPS Framework: Goals, Problems, and Solutions.

Goals

You'll need to first identify the goals for your business. Are you trying to increase sales? Are you focused on getting customers to buy more products at one time? Do you want more visitors to sign up for your newsletter?

Problems

Once you understand your goals, you need to understand what the problems are. What's keeping visitors from making repeat purchases? Why is it difficult to sign up for your newsletter? Identify which problems are the most important to address today.

Solutions

Now come up with ideas for how to solve these problems! Consider writing them as hypotheses: “We believe that if we do [the solution], we will impact [the problem]. We can tell this worked if [measurement] happens.”

Now keep doing this! You'll work through several solutions, then several problems, and ultimately you'll help your organization meet its goals.
