The Experimentation Methodology for SaaS Businesses

Before starting an experiment, make sure you understand why you're doing it. You can use the GPS Framework: Goals, Problems, and Solutions.

Goals

You'll need to first identify the goals for your SaaS marketing site. Are you trying to increase free trial signups? Are you focused on getting more visitors to sign up for your newsletter? Are you looking to get visitors to engage with more pages on your site?

Problems

Once you understand your goals, you need to understand what the problems are. What's keeping visitors from signing up for your free trial? What are visitors looking for and why can't they find it? Identify which problems are the most important to address today.

Solutions

Now come up with ideas for how to solve these problems! Consider writing them as hypotheses: “We believe that if we do [the solution], we will impact [the problem]. We can tell this worked if [measurement] happens.”

Now keep doing this! You'll work through several solutions, then several problems, and ultimately you'll help your SaaS product will meet its goals.
